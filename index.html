Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set my date of birth?

Open Settings (gear icon) and switch to the Setup tab. You can set your date of birth and life expectancy there.

What does Premium include?

Premium is a one-time purchase that unlocks all premium themes, unlimited chapters, and premium themes in widgets.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings, tap any premium theme to open the upgrade screen, then tap "Restore Purchase." Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

How do widgets work?

Long-press your home screen, tap the + button, and search for "A Life in Weeks." Widgets are free to use. Premium users can apply premium themes to their widgets.

Can I change the app language?

Yes! Go to Settings > Setup and select your preferred language. The app supports English, Spanish, Japanese, and Traditional Chinese.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data stays on your device. I do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
