Privacy Policy

A Life in Weeks

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

A Life in Weeks is designed with your privacy in mind. I do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

A Life in Weeks does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No account creation required
  • No data sent to external servers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Storage

All information you enter into the app — such as your date of birth, life expectancy, chapters, and theme preferences — is stored locally on your device only.

In-App Purchases

A Life in Weeks offers an optional one-time premium purchase. All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. I do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

I do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact me at forestoceanlife@gmail.com.
